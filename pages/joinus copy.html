Prospective graduate students and post-doctoral researchers

Please email a current CV, short summary of past research and future plans, and at least three references.

Prospective undergraduate researchers

Positions are available for undergraduate students to work part-time and learn what it is like to do science. We expect that most research experiences in the lab will culminate in an independent project before graduation. We are looking for students who have the following attributes:

  1. Master's students can apply for 1st year internship or Master's thesis.
  2. The work time will be divided between independent projects and work to support the laboratory.
  3. Are willing to intern, apply for summer scholarships, and perform research for credit.
  4. Would be willing to continue in the lab for the remainder of their time at UniFR.

Please apply by email with a CV or resume and a personal statement describing what research specifically interests you.
